Internal Tools

The virtues that build psychological resilience

1

Evaluative Thinking

Role: Epistemic Evaluator.
Practice: Classify every claim as True, False, or Partly True.
2

Sense-Making

Role: Reality Mapper.
Practice: Build explanations that survive new evidence.
3

Impartiality

Role: Bias Eliminator.
Practice: Use the "Veil of Ignorance" and Bayesian updating.
4

Self-Control

Role: Cognitive Regulator.
Practice: Default to delayed gratification; ignore distractions.
5

Fairness

Role: Guardian of Reciprocity.
Practice: Own up to every mistake to remove hypocrisy.
6

Perseverance

Role: Wielder of Fortitude.
Practice: Treat fear as a trainable response.
7

Self-Efficacy

Role: Builder of Identity.
Practice: View failure as a lack of skill, not a personal defect.
8

Persistence

Role: Keeper of Forward Motion.
Practice: Stack "tiny wins" during low-energy periods.
9

Moderation

Role: Resource Steward.
Practice: Give 80% energy to the few things that matter most.
10

Self-Reflection

Role: Experience Integrator.
Practice: Use heuristics: "If X happens, I will do Y".
