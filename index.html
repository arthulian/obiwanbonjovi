External Pressure

Understanding and countering psychological manipulation tactics

1

Cognitive Manipulation

Mechanism: Distorts reality through gaslighting and information control.
Impact: Erodes self-trust and causes chronic stress.
Evaluative Thinking Sense-Making
2

Persuasive Control

Mechanism: Emotional appeals and logical fallacies.
Impact: Exploits anxiety to force hasty decisions.
Impartiality Self-Control
3

Normative Control

Mechanism: Social pressure and conformity.
Impact: Fear of rejection and cognitive dissonance.
Fairness Self-Confidence
4

Overt Coercion

Mechanism: Threats, force, and exploitation.
Impact: Triggers intense fear and long-term trauma.
Perseverance Persistence
